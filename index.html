Hemophilia
Keep calm... and infuse Factor
Hemophilia is a medical condition where the blood fails to clot properly. This means that bleeds take longer time to heal, and have adverse consequences. Due to the absence of certain "clotting factors" in the blood stream, the clot that plugs the bleed fails to hold up, and it gives way, causing the bleed to recur. Clotting factors are certain proteins, and in Hemophilia, they are less in concentration compared to normal people.

>Hemophilia is generally found to be inherited from older generations. It might be prominent in the males, or recessive in the females. If the disorder is recessive in females, they are called "carriers" of the defective gene. But now, there are a lot of cases where the disorder is caused by spontaneous mutations, rendering the gene defective.

Frequent bleeds can derail any person's morale by restricting his range of activities. Then on, he has to face a wide array of issues on managing his health and work. If he proceeds deep into the work, without caring for health, he can land up in a serious problem, and can cause permanent damage to body parts. But if he has to discard the work and take care of health, the slow down can affect the mental strength adversely.
